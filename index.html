Is Year In Health free?
The core Year In Health experience is free. An optional Tip Jar is available for users who would like to support continued development.
Why do I not see any health data?
Make sure you have Apple Health data for the selected year and that the required Health permissions are enabled in Settings.
Does the app upload my Health data?
No. Health data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to a server.
Does Year In Health provide medical advice?
No. The app is intended for personal wellness insights and does not diagnose conditions or replace professional medical advice.
How can I share my recap?
Use the Year Story feature to generate an image from your data, then save it or share it through the iOS share sheet.
