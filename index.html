What the app does

Overview

Year In Health reads your Apple Health data (with your permission) and builds a clear summary of your current year or any past year.

  • See total yearly steps, distance and daily trends.
  • Review your average sleep and longest sleep streaks.
  • Track active calories and your most intense days.
  • View key heart rate stats: average, minimum and maximum.
  • Explore interactive charts for steps, sleep and activity.
  • Create a shareable “Year Story” image ready for social media.
  • Enjoy a New Year countdown and an animated recap experience.

Privacy & data

On-device

All data in Year In Health comes from Apple Health on your device. The app:

  • Does not create an online account.
  • Does not send your health data to external servers.
  • Only reads the data types you explicitly allow in Apple Health.
  • You can revoke access at any time in the Health app.

FAQ

Which data does Year In Health read?
The app can read steps, active energy, sleep analysis, workouts and heart rate from Apple Health, depending on what you approve in the permission screen.
Why don't I see any data?
Make sure you have Health data for the selected year, and confirm that Year In Health has permission in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.
I denied Health access by mistake. How can I fix it?
Open the Health app, tap your profile → Apps → Year In Health and enable the data types you want the app to use.
Does the app sync anything to a server?
No. All calculations and visualisations are done locally on your iPhone. Your health data never leaves your device.
