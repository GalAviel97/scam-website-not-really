Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Year In Health (“the App”) is developed by Gal Aviel. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. No Data Leaves Your Device

Year In Health does not send any personal or health data to external servers. All processing, calculations, charts and summaries happen locally on your iPhone.

2. Health Data Access

The App reads Apple Health data only after you explicitly grant permission in the Health app. Depending on your choice, the App may read:

  • Steps
  • Distance
  • Sleep Analysis
  • Active Energy
  • Workouts
  • Heart Rate

You can revoke access at any time under: Health App → Profile → Apps → Year In Health

3. No Tracking, No Ads

The App contains no analytics SDKs, advertising networks, trackers or cookies. Nothing is collected or stored outside your device.

4. User Profile & Account

The App does not require an account. Your name (if entered) is stored locally using App Storage and stays only on your device.

5. Data Storage

The App does not store or export your Health data. It only reads live data from the Apple HealthKit database.

6. TestFlight and Crash Reports

If you join the TestFlight beta, Apple may send anonymized crash logs to the developer. These logs contain no health data and cannot identify you.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. It does not collect or store personal information.
